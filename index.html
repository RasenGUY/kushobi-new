What is Kushobi

From the Author Nelly Blaise Half Haitian and the Dominican Republic, born in St. Maarten. Kushobi is a story based on Egyptian mythology and influenced by the Benin Kingdom, inclusive Japanese anime. The story has many concepts that are privy to voodoo and cosmic energy. It starts with Oxie, the voodoo priest from Benin and his path to becoming the political advisor in Egypt and the upholder of peace. Egypt has become a law-abiding nation using cosmic energy and voodoo to combat criminals. However, there has been reports of a secret society using dark energy, which is illegal. There is someone in the shadows working towards the demise of the law. Recently, Oxie has been investigating this forbidden sorcery. He began to suspect that the many citizens that were ill were correlated with these forbidden practices. Nevertheless, Oxie knows how he can cure these ailments, he need to go far east in Japan. His journey will lead him to an unexpected battle that will determine the future of Egypt. This is a prequel of a hero that is destined to avenge his father and restore peace in Egypt.
